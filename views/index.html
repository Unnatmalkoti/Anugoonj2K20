ABOUT US

Anugoonj which translates to “Echoing of small efforts” is the monumental annual cultural festival of GGSIPU which takes place in February.  Captivating all creative thoroughbreds from more than 100 affiliated colleges, the fest spans over three days. This extravaganza is escapism from the laborious college life. Anugoonj is the place to be whether, you want to flaunt your skills or stand with your mouth agape watching the talent or just an excuse to come and have tons of fun.
Anugoonj features events like dramatics, music, dance, quiz, debates, quiz, street play, and the most awaited event Mr and Ms Anugoonj among many others. If you have a talent, then we the platform to showcase it to a crowd of over 80000 viewers.
Anugoonj has previously hosted phenomenal artists like Pakshee Band,Kailash Kher, KK and many others. So get your groove on and be ready to be dazzled by the artists we bring this year!
