Classical Dance

Shastriya Nritiya or Indian Classical dance is well known all over the world. Not only is Classical Dance an integral part of the rich tapestry of Indian Culture but it’s also an excellent medium of expression for depicting mythological stories and tales as old as time.

If you possess the grace, poise and skill for Classical Dance then sign up for one of the most elegant competitions organised at Anugoonj.
